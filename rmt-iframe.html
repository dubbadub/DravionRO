Real Money Trading

This page contains information about Real Money Trading (RMT).

Dear Adventurers,

At Dravion RO, Real Money Trading (RMT) is Allowed.

We understand that many players spend countless hours hunting rare equipment, farming valuable items, and investing their time into the game. By allowing RMT, we want to give dedicated hunters and hardworking players the opportunity to turn their efforts into real value by selling the items they have legitimately obtained.

However, all RMT transactions are conducted entirely at your own risk. Please make sure you know and trust the person you are dealing with before sending any payment or in-game items.

We strongly encourage everyone to use the Official Admin Middleman Service whenever possible to ensure a fair and secure trading experience.

Thank you for supporting DravionRO and helping us build a safe, fair, and thriving community.

Official Middleman Service

To help make trades safer, you may request an Admin Middleman for your transaction.

Middleman Fee: 5% of the total transaction value.

Using an official middleman helps protect both the buyer and the seller by ensuring that:

The item and payment are verified before the trade is completed
Both parties receive what was agreed upon
The risk of scams is significantly reduced

The 5% middleman fee is used to help cover DravionRO's server expenses, including hosting, maintenance, and future improvements that keep the server running smoothly.

Trade Responsibly

If you decide to trade without using an official middleman, you accept all risks involved.

DravionRO Administration is NOT liable for:

Scams or fraudulent transactions
Lost money or in-game items
Chargebacks or payment disputes
Account theft or account-sharing incidents
Any disagreements between buyers and sellers

No refunds, reimbursements, restorations, or item recoveries will be provided for losses resulting from player-to-player RMT transactions.
