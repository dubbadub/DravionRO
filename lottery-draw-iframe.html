Lottery Draw

This page contains information about Lottery Draw.

HOW IT WORKS?

Command
@npc15
Purchase
Lottery Ticket
Choose
3 Numbers between 1 ~ 25
Draw
20:00 ST
Reward
Rodex/in-game mail
STEPS REQUIREMENTS
Purchase a Lottery Ticket using Credits 1 Lottery Ticket : 10 Credits
Choose your "own numbers" or "using lucky pick" 3 numbers from 1 ~ 25
The lottery will draw automatically Everyday around 20:00 Server Time
Winner will received the reward thru Rodex Must match all 3 numbers

JACKPOT WINNER

Multiple winners split the jackpot equally.

CONSOLATION PRIZE

Match 2 numbers and receive a consolation reward worth: 10% of the current jackpot
